If you've seen my Instagram then you'll know I'm a bit of a foodie and I'm slightly fixated on Vietnamese cooking lately. The reason for that is because it shares the comforts of French cooking (which I grew up on!) with the intricate Asian techniques and spices.

When I was out in Vietnam and speaking to the locals, I fell in love with their national pride, the importance they put on family and how mealtimes brought people together. The latter two things I can definitely relate to and it was fitting that on Father's Day I cooked Beef Pho(pictured above), summer Saigon prawn rolls and avocado ice cream in homage to these values.

Has to be said after the many hours of prep it was a huge success!

These recipes, by the way, are all from Uyen Luu's 'My Vietnamese Kitchen' cook book, and I would hugely recommend if you are ever in London a trip to her Supper Club (link here).

Happy cooking everybody!
